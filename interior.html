00. Background

Nearly 30% of the overall budget is for the design fee – a hefty price for perfection.

01. Field Research

Research Question: How might we streamline the process of interior design for general people?

Ideal System Process

The ideal process begins with client consultation and culminates in project management, ensuring each phase concludes definitively without the need for revisiting or revising previous stages.

Current System Mapping

However, in the true situation, it's typical to frequently revisit earlier stages, as evolving client preferences or situational changes often demand modifications to the initial design plan.

02. User Research

User Interviews

Interviewee 1

Q: What are the key challenges or frustrations you encounter when making interior design decisions?

A: Thins like not being able to show their own style and not talking enough with designers. Changing the design can be expensive, and it's hard to picture how everything will look in the end. Also, it's not always clear how much everything will cost.

Interviewee 2

Q: What common difficulties do you observe your clients facing when making design decisions?

A: Designers often find themselves spending more time on client consultations than actual design work, which can limit their creative process. Those with higher skill levels and recognition tend to work on more luxurious projects. Balancing client communication with design execution remains a key challenge

User Journey Map

By gathering information from interviews and observations, I aim to identify issues in the usual design process and understand what clients really want and need.

03. Competitive Analysis

Most AI-assist interior design are for professions, like designer and real estate. Either they are too complicated for general people to use, or they are not providing enough options for users to choose from.

04. Problem Defining

The pain points identified originated from the research I introduced earlier. Based on these insights, I converged my ideas into two separate concepts that each address different areas of the discovered pain points.
